Choose a makeup that is good for you and the environment!

The most important ingredients of beauty are sunshine, fresh air and clean water, which nature provides everyday. When we need a little help from makeups, let's not forget to select a product that is good for the nature who is so generous to us.

  • Cruelty Free

    When it comes to beauty and personal care, a "cruelty-free" brand is a brand that does not test its products on animals. A brand that is truly cruelty-free does not perform any animal testing on products or ingredients at any point during the making of their products. They also ensure that their suppliers don't test on animals, and no third-parties test on animals on their behalf.

  • Natural

    Natural cosmetics contain ingredients from natural sources, like plants or minerals.

  • Organic

    Organic cosmetics contain no arificial chemicals, hormones, antibiotics and GMOs.

  • Vegan

    Vegan cosmetics are made without ingredients produced by or derived from animals.
